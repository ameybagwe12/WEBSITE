Warli Art Painting

Warli painting is a form of tribal art mostly created by the tribal people from the North Sahyadri Range
in Maharashtra, India. This range encompasses cities such as Dahanu, Talasari, Jawhar, Palghar,
Mokhada, and Vikramgad of Palghar district. This tribal art was originated in Maharashtra,
where it is still practiced today.
